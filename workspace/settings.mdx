---
title: "Configure your CF0 account settings"
description: "Manage your account details, company watchlist, investment profile, and personal skills from the four tabs on the CF0 Settings page."
---

The Settings page is where you personalise CF0 to fit your workflow. Open it by clicking your avatar in the top-right corner and selecting **Settings**, or navigating directly to `/settings`. Settings are organised into four tabs: **Account**, **Companies**, **Profile**, and **Skills**.

## Account

The Account tab lets you update your personal details:

- **Name** — the display name visible to your teammates.
- **Email** — the address associated with your CF0 login. Email changes go through a verification step.
- **Profile picture** — upload or change your avatar. Your picture appears in team views and the dashboard.

## Companies

The Companies tab is your personal company watchlist. Companies you add here are available as context when you run research queries in Lab — CF0 can surface them automatically when you ask questions without specifying a ticker.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for a company">
    Type a company name or ticker in the search field. CF0 searches across 40,000+ companies in its filings database.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to your watchlist">
    Click **Add** next to any company. It appears immediately in your watchlist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove a company">
    Click the remove icon next to any company in your list to take it off your watchlist.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Add the companies you cover most frequently so Lab can reference them without you having to name them every time.
</Tip>

## Profile (Investment Profile)

The Profile tab lets you describe your investment mandate and focus areas. CF0 uses this information to personalise AI responses — Lab tailors its analysis, framing, and data emphasis to your stated strategy.

Fields you can set include:

- **Investment mandate** — for example, long/short equity, distressed credit, or growth equity.
- **Sector and geography focus** — the markets and industries you care about most.
- **Preferred metrics and benchmarks** — guide how CF0 frames comparisons and valuations.

<Note>
  Your investment profile is private to you. It is not shared with other members of your organisation.
</Note>

## Skills

The Skills tab shows the personal skills available in your account. Skills are reusable prompt templates that encode specific research workflows — for example, a one-click earnings analysis or a standardised due diligence checklist.

Here you can:

- **View** your active personal skills.
- **Enable or disable** skills without deleting them.
- **Delete** skills you no longer need.

Org-wide skills (shared across your team) are managed by an admin from the dedicated Skills page, not from this tab.

## Danger Zone

At the bottom of the Settings page is the **Danger Zone**. This section contains irreversible actions, including **Delete account**. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, query history, and personal data from CF0. Organisation data and shared documents are not affected.

<Note>
  Account deletion cannot be undone. If you want to leave an organisation without deleting your account, ask your org admin to remove your membership instead.
</Note>
