## How these notes were made

Each note was assembled in one Lab thread. The model framed the question, called filings and macro tools to pull the data, ran the per-name tearsheets through code-based templates (not AI inference), assembled the charts inline, and exported the polished PDF that became the Substack post. The full audit trail — every tool call, every figure with its source — is preserved with the thread. See [Citations and audit trail](/security/citations-and-audit) for how provenance is generated and exported.
