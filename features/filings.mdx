Filings gives you direct access to a curated archive of regulatory filings from companies around the world. You can search by company name or ticker, browse by market, open a specific filing, and navigate its sections — all without leaving CF0. Filing data is read from CF0's proprietary ingestion pipeline and updated regularly.

## Markets

The Filings landing page organises coverage into two tiers:

- **Filing ingestion available** — markets where CF0 has processed and indexed the full filing content, enabling section-level reading and full-text search. Brazil (BR) is the most recently added market.
- **Directory only** — markets where company and filing metadata is available for browsing, but full-text section search is not yet enabled.

Click any market in the grid to browse its companies.

## Search for a company

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Filings">
    Click **Filings** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search by name or ticker">
    Type a company name or ticker symbol into the search bar at the top. Results appear as you type across all markets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a company">
    Click a result to open the company's filing list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Browse by market

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the market grid">
    On the Filings landing page, click a market name — for example, **United States** — to open the company list for that market.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse companies">
    The company list shows each company's ticker, name, sector, and market cap. Click any row to view its filings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paginate">
    Use the **Previous** and **Next** controls at the bottom of the list to move through large markets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Read a filing

Once you select a company, its filing history appears as a table sorted by date. Each row shows the filing type (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, etc.), the filing date, and a link to the original document.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a filing">
    Click **Read** on any filing row. The section reader opens as a panel on the right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate sections">
    Use the section list in the reader to jump directly to any part of the filing — for example, Item 1A (Risk Factors) or Item 7 (MD&A).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search within the filing">
    Use the full-text search field inside the reader to find specific terms or passages within the document.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Filing data is sourced from CF0's proprietary pipeline, not fetched live from EDGAR on demand. Data is updated regularly, but there may be a short delay between a company filing with the SEC and its appearance in CF0.
</Note>
