CF0 connects to more than 25 live financial data sources so your research is always grounded in current, structured information — not stale training data. When Lab answers a question or generates a report, it pulls directly from these sources in real time and traces every number back to its origin.

## Citations on every data point

Every figure, metric, or claim that CF0 surfaces in Lab or a report includes a citation linking back to its exact source — the specific filing, transcript, data feed, or provider. You always know where a number came from.

<Tip>
  In Lab responses and generated reports, citations appear as inline references. Click any citation to open the underlying source document or data record directly.
</Tip>

## Data privacy

CF0 does not share your data with other organisations or use it to train AI models. Your research threads, documents, and reports are stored within your organisation's workspace and are accessible only to your team.

<Note>
  CF0's zero data retention policy means your data is never shared across organisations and is never used to improve or train the underlying AI models. Your research stays yours.
</Note>
