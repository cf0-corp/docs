CF0 can connect to external tools and data sources beyond its built-in 25+ financial feeds. Integrations let you bring data from third-party platforms into your CF0 workspace so Lab can reason over it alongside SEC filings, market data, and earnings transcripts.

<Info>
  The full Integrations settings panel is currently in preview and will be available to all users soon. If you need early access, contact the CF0 team directly.
</Info>

## Bringing external data into CF0 today

While the integrations UI is being finalised, the primary way to bring your own data into CF0 is by uploading documents directly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Documents page">
    Click **Documents** in the left navigation bar to open your workspace document library.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your files">
    Drag and drop files onto the upload area, or click **Upload** to browse your local files. CF0 supports:
    - **PDF** — research reports, pitch decks, agreements, scanned documents
    - **CSV** — financial models, data exports, cap tables
    - **XLSX** — Excel workbooks and structured data files
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask Lab about your documents">
    Once uploaded, your documents are immediately available as context in Lab. Ask questions like *"Summarise this pitch deck"* or *"What are the key terms in this agreement?"* and Lab will reference your uploaded file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Upload a company's investor presentation or a proprietary data export alongside CF0's live data sources to get analysis that combines both external and structured financial data in a single Lab response.
</Tip>

## What's coming in integrations

The integrations panel will let you connect CF0 to third-party tools via OAuth, so data from those platforms flows into your workspace automatically — without manual uploads. Integration setup will be done through **Settings → Integrations** once the panel is live.

<Note>
  When the Integrations panel launches, any connections you set up will be scoped to your organisation. All members of your org will be able to use connected data sources in Lab.
</Note>
