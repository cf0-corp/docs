## How to access filings

**Via the Filings page:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Filings page">
    Click **Filings** in the left navigation bar. The landing view shows all available markets and the total company count.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search or browse">
    Type a company name or ticker in the search bar to go directly to that company's filings. Alternatively, click a market to browse its full company list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a filing">
    Click any company to see its filing history. Select a specific filing to open the section viewer, where you can read and search individual sections.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Via Lab:**

You can also access filing data conversationally. Ask Lab questions like:

- *"What did Apple say about AI investment in their most recent 10-K?"*
- *"Show me Nvidia's revenue growth over the last five years."*
- *"Compare the debt covenants in these two credit agreements."*

Lab retrieves and cites the relevant sections automatically.

## Citations and traceability

Every number CF0 extracts from a filing is traceable to the exact filing, page, and section it came from. When Lab or a report surfaces a financial figure, the citation tells you:

- The company and filing type (e.g. 10-K, 10-Q)
- The filing date and period covered
- The specific section or table within the document

<Note>
  CF0's structured extraction achieves 90%+ accuracy on standard financial statements. For narrative sections (MD&A, risk factors), Lab reads the raw text directly and quotes it verbatim where precision matters.
</Note>
