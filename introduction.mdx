CF0 is an AI-native financial research platform built for family offices and hedge funds. It pulls from 25+ live data sources — including a proprietary SEC filing pipeline spanning 30 years — and lets your analysts ask complex research questions in plain language, generate branded PDF reports, and trace every number back to its original source. What used to take a full day of data gathering now takes minutes.

## How it works

1. **Ask a question in Lab.** Type a research question in plain language — for example, "Compare NVDA and AMD gross margins over the last four quarters." CF0 pulls data from the relevant sources, runs the analysis, and renders the results as structured components.
2. **Verify every number.** Each data point links back to its source — the exact filing, page, and section. Click any figure to see where it came from.
3. **Generate a report.** When you're ready to share findings, generate a branded PDF report directly from your Lab session. CF0 assembles the narrative, charts, and citations automatically.
4. **Save workflows as Skills.** Encode any repeatable research process as a skill so your whole team can run it in one click.

<Note>
CF0 never shares your data across organisations and never uses your research to train AI models. Access controls are enforced at the organisation level — your data stays within your workspace.
</Note>
