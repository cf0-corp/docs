cf0 accounts are invite-only. Once your firm is provisioned, sign-up is three steps. After that, you control role, sectors, coverage, and language from **Settings → Profile** at any time.

## Activate your invitation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invitation email">
    Click the sign-up link. It pre-fills your email and attaches you to your organisation automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a password">
    Enter your first name, last name, and a password. cf0 verifies the email address before activating the account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    cf0 walks you through role, sectors, coverage universe, and preferred language. Skip any optional step and revisit later via **Settings → Profile**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Organisations

Every cf0 account belongs to an organisation. There are no personal accounts — research, documents, reports, and skills live within the org workspace.

## Investment profile

Tells cf0 what you cover and how to frame answers. All settings live under **Settings → Profile**.

| Field | Options |
|---|---|
| Role | Analyst · Portfolio manager · Research operations · Other |
| Sectors | Technology · Healthcare · Financials · Energy · Consumer Discretionary · Consumer Staples · Industrials · Real Estate · Materials |
| Coverage | Tickers you follow — cf0 keeps their filings and data ready |
| Language | English · Portuguese · Spanish · French · German · Chinese — every response and report renders in this language |

Your investment profile is private to you. It isn't shared across the org.

## Invite your team

Org admins invite teammates from **Settings → Companies**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Companies">
    You'll see the team roster and any pending invitations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite (or bulk invite)">
    Click **Invite**, enter an email, choose a role:
    - **Member** — full feature access within the org
    - **Admin** — also manages members, billing, brand, integrations

    Use **Bulk invite** to paste multiple addresses at once with the same role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change roles or remove members">
    Use the role selector or the remove icon next to any member row.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For the full Settings surface (Memory, Brand, Integrations, Billing), see [Settings](/workspace/settings).
